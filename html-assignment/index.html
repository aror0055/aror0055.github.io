About Me

My name is Parth Arora, and I am currently in the IMD at Algonquin College. I am 20 years old, and I am an international student. I am from India and it is my 2nd year with Algonquin College. I switched my programs from CET-CS to IMD because I concluded that the program was not for me and I decided to switch to a more creative field. I studied science in high school, but I was never really into it, but I have been a photography enthusiast since my 15th birthday when my parents gifted me a camera.

I love photography and it has been an integral part of my life. This program interests me as I want to explore the creative side of my nature and make it into a career. I believe my next step will be to work in a company in the creative field of designing and content creation. I am also open to further education, but I would like to gain some industry experience before pursuing that route. I would love to work in some of the biggest names in the market one day.
